Technologies at the brink of our understanding

Quantum Computing

physics encompasses the study observables at all scales from the macro scale of cosmontology to the micro scale of subatomic. at different scales different frameworks dominate what is observed. in our everyday life what we observe is dominated by classical physics but at the subatomic level observations are dominated by quantum mechanics. as we attempt to better understand subatomic observations we can utilize the quantum framework to enhance the way we compute. computers as we know it are binary - communicating either 1 or 0. looking forward we may be able to control computation with more granularity allowing for newfound algorithms and calculations.

robotics

many of the ways that we learn and build are repetive and memorizable. as we continue to discover what we can automate and program we will dedicate these tasks to robotic agents. these agents are often optimized to a specefic goal and will aid humans in building and discovery.

Nanotechnology

imagine a robotic arm that can build a car. now imagine a robotic arm that can build an arm 1/5 of its size. now continue to build arms smaller and smaller until you are on the atomic level. at this level we can curate things we have never before been able to machine. creating at this level is nanotechnology and it has promise in many industries.
