Nanotechnology

Definition

Imagine a robotic arm that can build a car. Now imagine a robotic arm that can build an arm 1/5 of its size. Now continue to build arms smaller and smaller until you are on the atomic level. At this level we can curate things we have never before been able to machine. Creating at this level is nanotechnology and it has promise in many industries.
