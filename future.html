Quantum Computing

Physics encompasses the study of observables at all scales from the macro scale of cosmontology to the micro scale of subatomic. at different scales different frameworks dominate what is observed. in our everyday life what we observe is dominated by classical physics but at the subatomic level observations are dominated by quantum mechanics. as we attempt to better understand subatomic observations we can utilize the quantum framework to enhance the way we compute. computers as we know it are binary - communicating either 1 or 0 - quantum allows for a 3rd undetermined state. looking forward we may be able to control computation with more granularity allowing for newfound algorithms and calculations.
